Roboute Guilliman

Primarch of the 13th Legion

"The preachers preach of our victory as the galaxy burns."

Roboute Guilliman, also known as the "Avenging Son", is the Primarch of the Ultramarines Space Marine Legion which hailed from the Ultramar Galactic Sector of the Imperium of Man. He is the greatest logistic and administrative mind that the imperium has to offer.

List of Achievements

  • Already an accomplished warlord and diplomat before being discovered by the Emperor of Mankind
  • Leads the largest Space Marine Legion during the Great Crusade
  • Renowned for his efforts to preserve the Imperium in the aftermath of the devastating Horus Heresy
  • Authored the Codex Astartes
  • Created the modern Imperial Military Structure
